Introduction


Most people thought love stories are all about people falling in love with each other…



Most people thought love stories are all about getting the boy or girl they want…



Most people thought love stories are all about happy endings…



But love stories are not all about that isn’t it?

There are also love stories about people who are experiencing the CRUELLEST kind of love called UNREQUITED LOVE.Those love stories which the person doesn’t get the person they want to be with. Those love stories which the person fell in love alone. Those love stories which the person is stuck in one-sided love. Love stories are not all about having happy ending with the person you love; sometimes it is also about letting the person you love have his or her own happy ending with someone else.



Being in love with someone is an unexplainable feeling…



Not being loved back by the person you love hurts…



They say the best way to let the pain go is to let them go…



But how can we let go of someone who gave us a lot of memories to remember?



How can we let go of someone who wasn’t even ours in the first place?



How can we learn the art of letting go?
